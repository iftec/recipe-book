Here to Help

A place to add and store your very own recipes without them getting lost.

In your recipe book you can, add new recipes, view your recipes, add and view your favorites and search your recipes by ingredients or name.
You can even upload your own pictures for your recipes

To add a recipe to your recipe book:

  • Click on the Add Recipe tab, this will open the add recipe page.
  • Give your recipe a title.
  • Upload an image (if you have one) or a default one will be assigned.
  • Add the ingredients and the method for the recipe
    (The recipe method will be automatically numbered for you).
  • Add any notes if you wish.
  • Click on the Add Recipe button to add your recipe to your recipe book.

To view your recipes:

  • Click on the Your Recipes tab, this will open the recipes page.
  • You can add your recipe to your favorites list.
  • Edit your recipe.
  • Delete your recipe.
  • Print your recipe.

To view your favorites:

  • Click on the Your Favorites tab, this will open your favorites page.
  • You can remove a recipe from your list.
  • Print a recipe

Searching for recipes:

  • The search box will allow you to search for any recipe by name or ingredient.
  • There are a few recipes in the database to get you started.
  • You can add any recipe from the search results to your favorites list.
